Music Videos:



What is love?

The song "What Is Love?" by TWICE is a relatable anthem for anyone who has yet to experience love. It's about wondering what love is, and speculating on the exact feeling of being in love and experiencing it for the first time. The music and lyrics explore the emotions of anticipation, curiosity and excitement of finding love, while also addressing the fear of being alone. The song's chorus captures the sentiment of questioning what love is and the desire to eventually find and experience it. As the song progresses, the members of TWICE sing about wanting to know and understand what love feels like, and dreaming of it coming into their lives soon.



TT

"TT" by TWICE is a song about inner conflict and struggling with emotions. The chorus talks about the narrator's feelings of helplessness and frustration, asking why they can't do things the way they want to. The verses talk about being unable to control their emotions and being overwhelmed, as well as wanting some kind of comfort from the person they love. In the end, the narrator admits that they're like "TT" - seemingly discussing their hopelessness and confusion - and asks for their partner's love and support. This song speaks to the difficulty of expressing emotions, and the inevitability of having to push through difficult times.



LIKEY

"LIKEY" by TWICE is a song about being confident and declaring one's own worth. The song explores the idea of feeling uncertain and putting yourself out there, even if it feels intimidating. The chorus proclaims that "the words 'I like you' might be a cliche, but they don't fully express my feelings. Even if I can't sleep and can't come up with the words, that's enough." The chorus also talks about how twinges of self-doubt, especially with regards to one's physical appearance, can lead to hesitation. Through the song, TWICE encourages everyone to break the boundaries of self-doubt and embrace their inner beauty and worth.



FANCY

The song “FANCY” by TWICE describes a romantic attraction between two people. The lyrics compare their feeling to bright colors like tropical skies, red sunlight, and twinkling stars. They express their admiration for the other person, declaring their love and need for them and comparing it to a sweet indulgence. The chorus states, “You, I fancy you. I don’t want anybody else”, emphasizing that they only want the other person. Finally, the song closes by asking what will happen if one of them confesses their liking first. Overall, “FANCY” is a sweet, romantic song that portrays the love and admiration two people can feel for each other.



Cheer up

The song "Cheer Up" by TWICE is a love song encouraging their significant other to stay strong in their relationship. The song speaks to the struggles of dating someone in today's world, such as feeling shy around them, the pressure of having to respond quickly to text messages, and wishing the other person could understand the love the singer feels for them. The song's message is to assure the other person that they are worth the fight, it's okay to take things slow, and that the singer will wait for them to express their own love in their own way. The chorus brings the theme of the song together by asking the listener to take it easy, never give up, and take their time in showing their love.

About TWICE

TWICE (트와이스) is a girl group consisting of 9 members: Jihyo, Nayeon, Jeongyeon, Momo, Sana, Mina, Dahyun, Chaeyoung, and Tzuyu. The group debuted on October 20, 2015, through the survival show Sixteen, under JYP Entertainment. As of February 2020, JYPE is partnering with Republic Records to help promote TWICE worldwide.
